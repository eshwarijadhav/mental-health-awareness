Warning Signs

😔 Persistent Sadness

Feeling hopeless or emotionally empty for several weeks.

😞 Social Isolation

Withdrawal from friends, family, and favorite activities.

😴 Sleep Changes

Sleeping too much or too little may indicate distress.

⚠ Suicidal Thoughts

Talking about self-harm or feeling like a burden requires immediate support.
